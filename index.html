I am currently doing PhD at IIM Bangalore and my current research focus on developing exact algorithms to solve Integer Programming problems. Before joining IIMB, I earned my Bachelor's in Mining Engineering from IIT, Kharagpur. Outside of research, I enjoy playing badminton, and cricket.

Research Interests

Integer Programming

Developing exact algorithms to solve high-dimensional discrete decision making problems.

Cone Programming

Investigating the theoretical foundations and practical applications of cone programming to provide tight bounds for hard discrete problems.

Scheduling Algorithms

Designing scalable, deterministic, and stochastic scheduling algorithms for complex industrial, computational, and logistical systems.
